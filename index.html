Why do you need Nebulas AddressBook?
You can manage your address with AddressBook and send the account# to anyone you trust such as govenment agents. When you change your contact details via AddressBook, the agents will get the latest update via the account#. You save the hassle to contact each agencies for address change and you are controlling your contact information completely by yourself.
